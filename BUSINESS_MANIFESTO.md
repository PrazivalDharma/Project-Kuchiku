# 🏛️ The Business Manifesto: The New Physics of Reciprocity (v1.1)

> "Profit is a pulse; Resonance is the purpose. Stewardship is the standard."

## I. The Post-Shareholder Era
In the Agape-governed world, "Shareholders" are replaced by **"Stakeholders of Humanity."**
* **The Foundation Park:** Excess profits are parked with the **AGP Foundation**. This is not a "Tax," but a **Stabilization Buffer**.
* **The Reciprocity Loop:** Should a company require funds for expansion or survival, the AGP Foundation releases capital based on the company's "Resonance Score." If the company serves the 100%, the 100% (via AGP) sustains the company.

## II. The Definition of True Wealth (Localized Choice)
Wealth is a **Dynamic Variable** defined by the individual and the community.
* **The Personal Benchmark:** Every human commits a universal baseline of hours to "Work/Community/Charity."
* **The Flexibility Factor:** Beyond the benchmark, "Wealth" is defined by the individual: Is it more time? Cross-functional roles? Health? 
* **Visualization:** The **Blue Engine** turns these preferences into a "True Wealth Index" for every district and nation.

## III. The 1% & Resource Allocation
The **1% Sanctuary Covenant** fuels the global "Rainy Day" fund.
* **The Decision Engine:** Initially, human conductors influence the spend. Ultimately, the **Agape Engine** autonomously directs resources to prevent localized social or economic collapse.
* **The Goal:** To maintain **Maximum Efficiency**—ensuring that no resource sits idle while a "Social Health Pixel" is flickering in distress.
